Chilli Con Carne

Ingredients

  • Beef mince 500g
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Fresh chilli
  • Kidney beans
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Tin of chopped tomatoes

Steps

  1. Brown the onions
  2. Add the mince and brown it
  3. Add garlic, chilli, and spice
  4. Cook for few minutes
  5. Add Kidney beans and tin of tomatoes and simmer for 30mins
  6. Serve with rice
